Explore Cairo's Best Dining Spots

Uncover Cairo's hidden culinary treasures beyond the beaten path

Hidden Culinary Gems of Cairo

Al Khal Egyptian Restaurant

Location: Zamalek, Cairo

Cuisine: Traditional Egyptian

Al Khal offers a cozy ambiance with authentic Egyptian flavors. From hearty koshary to tender lamb, this is a favorite among locals.

La Trattoria

Location: Downtown Cairo

Cuisine: Italian

A classic Italian dining spot with a relaxed atmosphere. Known for its handmade pasta and selection of fine wines.

Zooba

Location: Maadi, Cairo

Cuisine: Modern Egyptian Street Food

Zooba combines traditional street food with a modern twist. Try their take on taameya and hawawshi for a unique experience.

Fasahet Somaya

Location: Garden City, Cairo

Cuisine: Home-style Egyptian

A tiny, tucked-away restaurant where chef Somaya serves daily-changing home-cooked meals. Seating is limited, and the menu depends on local market ingredients. A true locals' secret.

Tabali

Location: Zamalek, Cairo

Cuisine: Modern Egyptian Fusion

Nestled in a quiet street, Tabali offers innovative Egyptian cuisine using locally sourced ingredients. Their menu reimagines traditional dishes with contemporary techniques.

Sharkiya Grill

Location: Old Cairo

Cuisine: Traditional Grilled Meats

A small, unassuming grill house known only to locals. Specializes in perfectly seasoned, slow-cooked meats and traditional side dishes. No frills, just exceptional flavor.

Abou El Sid

Location: Zamalek, Cairo

Cuisine: Authentic Egyptian

A hidden gem that captures the nostalgic essence of old Cairo. Offers traditional dishes like mahshi, molokhia, and exceptional stuffed pigeon in a vintage setting.
